General Information

A few words about the TSMC Museum of Innovation

Innovation originates from an idea, yet it has the potential to introduce unimaginable transformation to billions of people worldwide!

The TSMC Museum of Innovation is located at the global headquarters of TSMC, next to the first floor of Fab 12 in the Hsinchu Science Part and occupies over 170 ping.

The TSMC Museum of Innovation contains three galleries: A World of Innovation, Unleashing Innovation, and Dr. Morris Chang, TSMC Founder. Through interactive technology, digital content, and historical data, visitors can see how integrated circuits are everywhere in out daily lives, how continuing innovation makes out lives more rich and exciting, and how continuing progress in science and technology opens up unlimited possibilities. At the same time, the exhibits show the contributions that TSMC has made to global integrated circuit innovation and Taiwan’s economic development.
