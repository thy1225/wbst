FAQs

Where is the TSMC Museum of Innovation? What is the address and contact information?

The TSMC Museum of Innovation is located at the global headquarters of TSMC, next to the first floor of Fab 12 in the Hsinchu Science Park. Address: No. 1, Lixing 7th Rd., Hsinchu Science Park, Phone: (03)5631968, E-mail: service@tsmcmuseumofinnovation.com.tw, Website:http://www.tsmcmuseumofinnovation.com。

Do I need to buy a ticket for admission to the TSMC Museum of Innovation? Do I need to make a reservation?

At present, admission to the TSMC Museum of Innovation is free but by reservation only. Please go to the Online Reservation page of the museum’s website to make reservations.

Reminder: Online reservations can be made 24 hours a day. Reservations can be made one month in advance beginning on the first day of the month to the day before the reservation date. Same-day reservations will not be accepted.

When is the TSMC Museum of Innovation open?

The TSMC Museum of Innovation is open at the following times:
Monday~Friday: 09:00~17:00 Saturday: 10:00~17:00
Closed: Sundays, national holidays, and other days that will be officially announced

At present, admission to the TSMC Museum of Innovation is available by reservation only. Please go to the Online Reservation page on the museum’s website to make reservations.

Reminder: Online reservations can be made 24 hours a day. Reservations can be made one month in advance beginning on the first day of the month to the day before the reservation date. Same-day reservations will not be accepted.

How can I confirm that my reservation has been made successfully?

After you complete your reservation, you will see the message “You have successfully completed your reservation!” The system will also send a confirmation to your e-mail address. Please print out or take a screenshot of your reservation form (including the reservation code) and present the printout or screenshot to museum personnel upon entry. We also suggest that you use the View/Cancel Reservation function after making your reservation to check whether your reservation time is correct.

Reminder: We do not send individual notifications regarding unsuccessful logins and queries caused by incorrect information given during the reservation process. If you cannot log in to view your reservation, please call our service line (03)5631968.

What do I do if I have forgotten my online reservation code?

You can look up your reservation code on our website’s reservation system, which is available 24 hours a day.

Reminder: We do not send individual notifications regarding unsuccessful logins and queries caused by incorrect information given during the reservation process. If you cannot log in to view your reservation, please call our service line (03)5631968.

How do I cancel my reservation in the event that I cannot make it?

If you cannot make it to the museum at the time of your reservation, please cancel your reservation online before noon on the day prior to the reserved date.To protect the convenience of other visitors, users who fail to show at the reserved time 3 times without prior notice or cancelation beforehand will be unable to make reservations in the future, and any other reservations made will be cancelled automatically.

Reminder:The cancel function will not be available in the online reservation system after noon on the day before the reserved date. Please make cancellations before that time. Thank you for your cooperation.

Can I hold my reservation? How long can I hold it for?

A visit takes about 90 minutes, including a 30- to 40-minute guided tour. Each visit must begin and end on time for the sake of the next group of visitors. If you cannot arrive on time, your visit will be shortened. We apologize for any inconveniences.

Do I need to check-in before my visit? What documents do I need to bring? How early do I have to come?

Yes. Please bring your printout or screenshot of your reservation form (including the reservation code) and present it to museum personnel upon entry. Also, we suggest that you arrive at the entrance 10 minutes ahead of time.

Is there an age restriction for visitors?

We welcome visitors of all ages. However, to maximize visit quality, please do not eat, drink, shout, run, or lie down on seats in the museum.

How do I apply for group visits? Is there a limit to the number of people in a group?

At present, admission to the TSMC Museum of Innovation is available by reservation only. We apologize for any inconvenience. For parties containing between 1 and 5 people, please select “Individual” on the Online Reservation page on the museum’s website. For parties containing between 6 and 15 people, please select “Group”. For parties containing more than 15 people, please fill out the Large Group Tour Request Form.

Are there guided tours? What languages are the guided tours available in?

Each visit includes a guided tour. Mandarin Chinese and English guided tours are available. We apologize for any inconvenience if your language is not represented here.

How do I get to the TSMC Museum of Innovation? Is there parking available nearby?

Please refer to the Transportation Guide for transportation (including transfers), parking, and map information. Currently, the TSMC Museum of Innovation provides two free parking lots (Note: hyperlink) for visitors. However, there are a limited number of parking spaces, and we cannot guarantee vacancy. Please note that your vehicle may only enter the parking lot 30 minutes before the time of your reservation. Please submit your reservation form to parking personnel at the parking lot entrance. After your visit, please drive your vehicle out of the parking lot within 30 minutes. If the parking lots are full, please find a parking space in the area surrounding the museum. We apologize for any inconvenience. Thank you for your cooperation.

Are eating and drinking allowed in the museum?

To maximize visit quality, please do not eat, drink, smoke, chew gum, or chew betel nuts within the museum.

: Are there facilities for people with reduced mobility?

The museum is equipped with accessible ramps and restrooms.

Are there any facilities for babies?

The museum has an accessible restroom that contains a sink and diaper changing table. There is also hot and cold drinking water available for visitors with infants.

Are there baby stroller or wheelchair rentals available?

Due to limited space, we do not provide baby stroller or wheelchair rentals. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Are pets/guide dogs allowed in the museum?

Guide dogs on duty are allowed in the museum, but no other pets are allowed on the premises. We suggest that visitors with pets make reservations for pet sitting at a pet hotel nearby.

Are sign language or braille guided tours available?

At present, there are no sign language or braille guided tours available in the museum. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Is photography or video recording allowed in the museum?

Although photography is allowed in the museum, flash photography, selfie sticks, monopods, tripods, and video or audio recording are not permitted. We apologize for any inconvenience. We also encourage visitors to take advantage of our group photo spot located in front of the museum’s entrance.

Is there a spot for group photos available?

There is a group photo spot located in front of the museum’s entrance. We encourage visitors to take advantage of it. Also, although photography is allowed in the museum, flash photography, selfie sticks, monopods, tripods, and video or audio recording are not permitted. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Are the exhibits in the museum permanent? Will there be any special exhibitions?

The exhibits currently displayed in the museum are permanent exhibits. There are no special exhibitions at this time. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Does the museum have a check room/lockers?

There are some small storage lockers in the museum. Upon entry into the museum, please check any backpacks, travel bags, luggage, or full-size umbrellas larger than A4 size paper in the check room.

Can I wear slippers/flip-flops/sandals into the museum?

We ask that visitors refrain from wearing slippers/flip-flops/sandals into the museum. Thank you for your cooperation.

Is there Wi-Fi available in the museum?

There are no Wi-Fi services available in the museum. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Does the museum sell any souvenirs?

The museum does not sell any souvenirs. We apologize for any inconvenience.
