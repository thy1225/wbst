Online Reservation

Online reservations can be made for reservations starting the next day to the end of the next month. Same-day reservations will not be accepted.

To protect interests of each visitor, an individual can only make one reservation per month.

For school groups with more than 15 people, please fill out the School Group Tour Reservation Form.
For other groups with more than 15 people, please fill out the Large Group Tour Reservation Form.
