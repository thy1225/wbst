Online Reservation

Online reservations can be made 24 hours a day. Reservations can be made one month in advance beginning on the first day of the month to the day before the reservation date. Same-day reservations will not be accepted.

When the system is busy after 00:00 on the 1st of each month, notification mail will be delayed for 30 minutes. We apologize for any inconveniences.
