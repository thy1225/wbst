Terms and Conditions & Privacy Policy

Welcome to the TSMC Museum of Innovation’s Online reservation System. To protect your personal information, clicking the “Confirm reservation” button indicates that you agree to provide your personal information to TSMC for the purpose of making a reservation at the TSMC Museum of Innovation. Before you make your reservation, please carefully read the following Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy to ensure your visitor rights and visit quality as well as those of other visitors. Thank you!

Terms and conditions:

  1. For a school group with more than 15 people, please fill out the School Group Tour Reservation Form. For other groups with more than 15 people, please fill out the Large Group Tour Reservation Form.
  2. The services provided by this Online Reservation System include making, viewing, and canceling reservations and providing visitor information.
  3. Online reservations can be made for reservations starting the next day to the end of the next month. Same-day reservations will not be accepted.
  4. In the event of cancelation, the system will automatically display the quota available. The system does not hold reservations or have waitlists.
  5. Your reservation is only successful if the reservation completion page displays your reservation code. Please take advantage of the View/Cancel Reservation function to confirm that your request has been successful!
  6. Entering the Museum indicates that you agree to all of the above-mentioned terms and conditions. Visitors shall not cause any damage or destruction to Museum's exhibits or facility. Users meeting any of the following conditions will not be able make reservations.
    1. The user fails to show up at the reserved time without prior notice or cancelation beforehand for 3 or more times.
    2. The user makes numerous reservations and then cancels numerous reservations, thereby affecting the rights of other visitors.
    3. The user resells the reservation code for commercial profit.
    4. The user violates visitor policies during their visit and does not heed reminders or warnings.
