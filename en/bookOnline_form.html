Online Reservation

Reservations can be made for all opening hours one month in advance beginning on the first day of the month to the day before the reservation date. Same-day reservations will not be accepted.

To protect the rights of other visitors, each person may only make one reservation per month.

For school groups with more than 15 people, please fill out the School Group Tour Reservation Form.
For other groups with more than 15 people, please fill out the Large Group Tour Reservation Form.
