Grand Opening of the TSMC Museum of Innovation

2016/12/01

The TSMC Museum of Innovation ("The Museum"), located next to TSMC’s global headquarters and Fab 12A in the Hsinchu Science Park, today held its grand opening. Admission to the TSMC Museum of Innovation is free. At present, reservations are required. To make your reservations, please go to the Online Reservation page of the museum’s website.

The Museum encompasses three exhibition galleries: "A World of Innovation", "Unleashing Innovation", and "Dr. Morris Chang, TSMC Founder", with a floor area of approximately 562 square meters. Through interactive technology, digital content, and historical documents we will learn about the pervasiveness of ICs in our daily lives and about their continued advancement. In addition, we will learn how ICs are making our lives more fulfilling and how they are driving technology beyond our imagination. We will also learn how TSMC contributes to global IC innovation and to Taiwan's economy.

The TSMC Museum of Innovation invites you to explore the history behind innovations in integrated circuits, and how these innovations impact our daily lives!
