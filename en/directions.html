Directions

By car

National freeways

National Freeway No. 1
Exit National Freeway No. 1 at 95B-Hsinchu. Take Yuanqu 1st Road to Lixing 6th Road and drive to Lixing 7th Road.

National Freeway No. 3
Exit National Freeway No. 3 at 98-Baoshan, and drive toward the Science Park. Take Kehuan Road to Yuanqu 3rd Road and drive to Lixing 7th Road.

Provincial Highways

Take Provincial Highway 1 to Sec. 2 of Dongda Road in Hsinchu City, and then take County Highway No. 122 to Guangfu Road. Continue on Jieshou Road to Lixing 6th Road and then drive to Lixing 7th Road.

Taiwan High Speed Rail

After getting off at the Hsinchu THSR Station, you can choose on of the following ways to get to the TSMC Museum of Innovation:

Taxi

A taxi trip to the entrance of the TSMC Museum of Innovation takes 25 minutes (during non-rush hour periods), and the fare is approximately NTD 250 (depending on traffic conditions).

Free bus + walking

You can take the Orange Line of the free Hsinchu Science Park Shuttle Buses and get off at the Life Hub, which is a 30-minute trip (during non-rush hour periods). From there, it’s a 25-minute walk to the TSMC Museum of Innovation.

For information on the Orange Line of the Hsinchu Science Park Shuttle Buses, please refer to the link below:
http://www.sipa.gov.tw/home.jsp?serno=201001210045&mserno=201001210037&menudata=ChineseMenu&contlink=content/traffic_4.jsp&serno3=201001210049

Taiwan Railway

After getting off at the Hsinchu Railway Station, you can choose on of the following ways to get to the TSMC Museum of Innovation:

Taxi

A taxi trip to the entrance of the TSMC Museum of Innovation takes 25 minutes (during non-rush hour periods), and the fare is approximately NTD 250 (depending on traffic conditions).

Bus + walking

Walk to the bus stop on Minzu Road (next to SOGO Department Store and diagonally opposite Kuan-nan Wholesale. You take Bus 0010_Blue 1 (fare: NTD 15; half fare: NTD 8) or Bus 5608 Hsinchu－Xiagongguan (fare: NTD 24; half fare: NTD 12) to World Senior High School. On either bus, it’s a 25-minute trip (during non-rush hour periods).

After getting off at to World Senior High School, keep walking forward past Hotel Royal. Turn right on Jieshou Road and then keep walking straight to Lixing 6th Road and then turn right on Lixing 7th Road. It’s a 15-minute walk.

For information on Bus 0010_Blue 1 , please refer to the link below:

For information on Bus 5608 Hsinchu－Xiagongguan, please refer to the link below:
