Getting Here

Directions

By car

National Freeways

National Freeway No. 1
Exit National Freeway No. 1 at 95B-Hsinchu. Take Park Ave. 2 to Li Hsin Rd. 6 and drive to Li Hsin Rd. 7.

National Freeway No. 3
Exit National Freeway No. 3 at 98-Baoshan, and drive toward the Science Park. Take Kehuan Rd. to Park Ave. 3 and drive to Li Hsin Rd. 7.

Provincial Highways

Take Provincial Highway 1 to Dongda Rd. Sec. 2 in Hsinchu City, and then take County Highway No. 122 to GuangFu Rd. Continue on Jieshou Rd. to Li Hsin Rd. 6 and then drive to Li Hsin Rd. 7.

Taiwan High Speed Rail

After getting off at the Hsinchu THSR Station, you can choose one of the following ways to get to the TSMC Museum of Innovation:

Taxi

Approximately 25 minutes (during non-rush hour periods), and the fare is approximately NTD 250 (depending on traffic conditions).

Free bus + walking

You can take the Orange Line of the free Hsinchu Science Park Shuttle Buses and get off at the HSP Link (Life Hub), which is a 30-minute trip (during non-rush hour periods). From there, it’s a 25-minute walk to the TSMC Museum of Innovation.

For information on the Orange Line of the Hsinchu Science Park Shuttle Buses, please refer to the link below:
http://www.sipa.gov.tw/english/home.jsp?serno=201003210015&mserno=201003210003&menudata=EnglishMenu&contlink=content/introduction_2_4.jsp&serno3=201003210023#7

Taiwan Railway

After getting off at the Hsinchu Railway Station, you can choose one of the following ways to get to the TSMC Museum of Innovation:

Taxi

Approximately 25 minutes (during non-rush hour periods), and the fare is approximately NTD 250 (depending on traffic conditions).

Bus + walking

Walk to the bus stop on Minzu Rd. (next to SOGO Department Store). Take Bus 0010_Blue 1 (fare: NTD 15; half fare: NTD 8) or Bus 5608 Hsinchu－Xiagongguan (fare: NTD 24; half fare: NTD 12) to World Senior High School. On either bus, it’s a 30-minute trip (during non-rush hour periods).

After getting off at to World Senior High School, keep walking forward past Hotel Royal Hsinchu. Turn right on Jieshou Rd. and then keep walking straight to Li Hsin Rd. 6 and then turn right on Li Hsin Rd. 7. It’s a 15-minute walk.

For information on Bus 0010_Blue 1 , please refer to the link below:
http://www.hcbus.com.tw/big5/information-2.asp

For information on Bus 5608 Hsinchu－Xiagongguan, please refer to the link below:
