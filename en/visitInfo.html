＊Please take advantage of our tour reservation services to maximize the quality of your visit.

Visitor Policies

  1. Please do not eat, drink, smoke, chew gum, or chew betel nuts within the museum.
  2. Please do not vandalize any exhibits or gallery equipment. Please do not shout, run, or lie down on seats in the museum.
  3. Pets are not permitted in the museum (except guide dogs).
  4. Please check any backpacks, travel bags, luggage, or full-size umbrellas larger than A4 size paper in the check room.
  5. Although photography is allowed in the museum, flash photography, selfie sticks, monopods, tripods, and video or audio recording are not permitted.
  6. Please note that your vehicle may only enter the parking lot 30 minutes before the time of your reservation. Please submit your reservation form to parking personnel at the parking lot entrance. After your visit, please drive your vehicle out of the parking lot within 30 minutes as well. If the parking lot is full, please find a parking space in the area surrounding the museum. We apologize for any inconveniences. Thank you for your cooperation.
