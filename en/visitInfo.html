＊Please note that reservations are required for admission to the TSMC Museum of Innovation at present. To make your reservations, please go to the Online Reservation page of the museum's website.

Visitor Policies

To ensure a safe, enjoyable museum experience for all visitors, please be sure to comply with the following regulations:

  1. All visits to TSMC Museum of Innovation (the "Museum") are by reservation only. All individual and group visits must complete reservations prior to arrival.
  2. Visitors shall not cause any damage or destruction to Museum's exhibits or facility. Visitors shall also refrain from speaking too loudly, running, or laying on benches. Please keep proper visit etiquette at all time.
  3. Visitors are expected to wear appropriate attires when visiting the Museum. You are recommended to wear comfortable shoes when visiting, and please do not wear slippers inside the Museum.
  4. Please set your cellphones or other audible devices to silent or vibrate mode while in the Museum.
  5. Dangerous items are strictly prohibited inside the Museum. No food, beverages, pets (except guide dogs), illegal or harmful substances are allowed inside the Museum. Anything larger than a A4 paper size, visitors must store such items in lockers. Visitors are advised to keep any valuable personal items on themselves at all times.
    ※There are umbrella racks located at the Museum entrance for visitors to place their umbrellas before entering the Museum.
  6. No eating, drinking, smoking, chewing gum, betel nut, or alcohol inside the Museum.
  7. NO videotaping or sound recording inside the Museum is allowed. Selfie sticks and tripods are also prohibited.
  8. Due to safety reasons, no roller skates, skateboards or bicycles are allowed inside the Museum.
  9. The Museum reserves the right to refuse admission to visitors who are deemed likely to cause damages or disturb order inside the Museum.
  10. The above regulations are subject to change, so please refer to the onsite bulletin board for any updates.
  11. Please note that your vehicle may only enter the parking lot 30 minutes before the time of your reservation. Please submit your reservation form to parking personnel at the parking lot entrance. After your visit, please drive your vehicle out of the parking lot within 30 minutes as well. If the parking lot is full, please find a parking space in the area surrounding the Museum. We apologize for any inconveniences. Thank you for your cooperation.
